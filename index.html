About Me

Kory was born in Houston, Texas, but he grew up in Phoenix, Arizona. He attented the University of Arizona while studying public health. During his time there, he started a club doing volunteer work in rural communities in Central America, completed an intership at the Pima County Department of Environmental Quaility, and learned American Sign Language (ASL). He is currently emplyed at the Arizona Department of Environmental Quailty, and has a passion for keeping our earth clean and healthy.
